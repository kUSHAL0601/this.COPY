A free tool to make life of codechef users a bit simpler

The Introduction

So in case you were wondering what this is all about ...

The whole idea came up with codeforces providing the copy paste feature and understanding how helpful it could be. Hence a tool to help you copy test cases on codechef with a click.

How to use it

Have a browser?

It is very simple to use. It is platform independent. All you need to do it drag and drop the tool button below to your bookmarklet bar and simply click on it whenever you want to use it. A copy button will be available on the codechef page. Use it to copy your test case! You could also simply bookmark the link and click on the bookmark whenever you want to use it. You can also use browser specific tools available. For plugins you may have to refresh the page after useing it.
